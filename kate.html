Kate O'Connor

My name is Kate O’Connor and I am a third-year Media Production student with a strong interest in branding, advertising, and marketing. Throughout my time on the course, I have developed skills across a range of creative software including Adobe Premiere Pro, Illustrator, and Photoshop, which I use to produce both video and design-based work.
My practice focuses on creating engaging visual content that communicates a clear identity or message. I enjoy leading creative projects and collaborating with others, taking responsibility for shaping ideas and guiding them from concept through to final production.
Alongside my studies, I enjoy producing my own YouTube videos, which allows me to experiment with content creation, editing, and audience engagement. This personal work helps me continue developing my creative voice while exploring how digital media can connect with audiences online.
In the future, I hope to continue developing my skills within creative media, particularly in areas that combine branding, advertising, and digital content production.
