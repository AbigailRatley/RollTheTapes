Sam Poge

My work focuses on videography, photography and graphic design, with a strong interest in creating content for music and event brands. I have developed good skills using Photoshop, where I create graphics and promotional visuals that work alongside video content and help artists build their online presence.
Alongside my studies, I run my own creative brand, Poge Productions, where I work with DJs, collectives and brands to produce content for their social media. This includes filming events, editing short-form videos and creating graphics that capture the energy of nightlife and music culture.
Through this work I have gained experience working in fast paced environments and creating content that fits different platforms and audiences.
In the future I am looking to move more into commercial based filming, working with brands and businesses to create professional promotional content and visual campaigns.
