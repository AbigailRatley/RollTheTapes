Roll The Tapes

Nottingham Trent third year Media Production students are proud to present our showcase event celebrating the culmination of three years of creative development, collaboration and professional practice.
Our game show style nights aims to encourage interaction and light-hearted competitiveness, whilst showing you all our work.
At the centre of the evening our live gameshow segment will start, hosted by two members of the cohort (trust me, you will not be disappointed). Audience members will be invited to participate in media-themed challenges, including “What Happens Next?”, where viewers watch a short clip and predict how the narrative unfolds.
Alongside the live entertainment, a curated static exhibition will showcase a range of final-year projects, including short films, documentaries, branded content, photography, and graphic design work. This space allows visitors to explore our creative processes and professional portfolios in more depth.
This showcase represents more than the end of our degree — it marks our transition into the media industry. We warmly invite professors, students, prospective applicants, friends and family to join us in celebrating the creativity, innovation and ambition of our final-year cohort.

Timeline:

16:00- Doors open
17:30- Gameshow Starts
19:00- Event finishes
There will be refreshments available throughout and films in the main lecture theatre from 16:00.
