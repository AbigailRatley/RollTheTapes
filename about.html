About Us

On our BA Media Production course, we’ve developed the creative and technical skills needed to thrive in today’s fast-moving media landscape. Media is everywhere, and throughout our time on the course we’ve learned not just how to consume it, but how to create work that stands out and makes an impact. We’ve gained hands-on experience across film, television, audio and digital design, working with industry-standard tools to bring our ideas to life. From pitching concepts and directing shoots to editing, sound design and branding, we’ve had the opportunity to explore every role within a production team and understand how collaboration drives successful media projects. Working on live briefs and building our own portfolios has given us real-world insight and professional confidence. As a cohort, we’ve grown creatively and technically, and we’re leaving the course with a body of work we’re proud of and the skills to take into the media industry.
