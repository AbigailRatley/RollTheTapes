Anna Justice

I’m Anna Justice, a 21-year-old freelance photographer and videographer based in Nottingham, UK, currently in my third year studying Media Production. My work is centered around music and the atmosphere it creates, with a focus on capturing honest moments, emotion, and energy through both still and moving image.
Through my degree, I’ve developed my practice across modules in short film, branding, and client-led, which have shaped how I approach storytelling, visual identity, and audience engagement. These skills feed directly into my freelance work, allowing me to balance creativity with a clear sense of purpose and professionalism.
Alongside my studies, I’ve worked across live music, music videos, promotional shoots, and behind-the-scenes content. My work has been published by Taped Magazine and Groupchat Mag, and in 2025 I toured with Paradise Circus on their Welcome to Paradise Tour. Looking ahead, I’m expanding into events and artist management through the launch of Hideout Records, a creative agency and record label formed with close collaborators.
